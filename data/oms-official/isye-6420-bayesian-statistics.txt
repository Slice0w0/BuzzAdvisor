Before Taking This Class...
Suggested Background Knowledge
Students should have taken introductory courses in probability in statistics. They should also have basic programming proficiency and be comfortable with calculus.
Technical Requirements and Software
High-speed internet connection
Laptop or desktop computer with a minimum of a 2 GHz processor and 2 GB of RAM
Windows for PC computer or Mac iOS for Apple computers
